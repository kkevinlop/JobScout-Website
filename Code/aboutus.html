ABOUT US

With over 20 years of extensive corporate, agency, international, and campus recruitment experience, we are confident in bringing our diverse and vast talent acquisition expertise to solve your staffing needs. Our roots are from Western Canada and have a strong track record of successfully placing thousands of candidates over the years locally, but our strength truly is in our connections and perseverance and can provide our recruiting experience across the globe.
